My story so far

I was born in Durban, South Africa now currently living in London. After finishing school and getting my diploma in Multimedia and Graphic Design I started working for a new media company called Ion Access, this later became Immedia. After 6 years with the company I decided to move to London where I joined the Walt Disney Company where I am the Lead Designer for disney.co.uk.
